Welcome to the Bulk Search & Replace tool for WordPress CLI users!

Congratulations! You're running the Bulma start project.
It includes everything you need to build your own website with Bulma.

How to use this tool:

In the Boxes below, insert the domains present on your mixed content errors (you can also use a tool like WhyNoPadlock to scan your site to give you a log of those errors too.

Now in the box below, input the primary domain you are using on Flywheel (this is the domain that you have clicked "Mark as Primary" if you arent sure on what you have selected; check your dasbboard or check out our Going Live Documentation.
